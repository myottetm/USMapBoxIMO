Gentrification and Low Income Concentration
in U.S. Census Tracts, 2000 to 2016

Gentrifying tracts are defined as those experiencing a decline in low-income population and low-income population share, without experiencing heavy population loss. Tracts with concentrating poverty are those experiencing an increase in low-income population and low-income population share, without experiencing rapid population growth.
